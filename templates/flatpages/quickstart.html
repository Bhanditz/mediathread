Mediathread Quickstart Guide

Mediathread is a platform for independent and collaborative analysis of and composition with online multimedia content.

Get the Big Picture

If you are using Mediathread in a course, your instructor may give you materials to review or assignments to complete within the Mediathread environment.

Import then Excerpt Media

Images and videos from YouTube, Flickr, library databases, course libraries and numerous other online sources can be imported into the Mediathread analysis environment. Once in Mediathread, media items can be excerpted, annotated and organized.

Compose Analyses and Embed Your Excerpts

Media items and excerpts can also be embedded into “Mediathreads:” discussions, essays, and other written analyses.

Review and Discuss Completed Work

“Mediathreads” may be published so that they are available for viewing and commentary by your instructor or your classmates. You can also generate a publicly viewable URL.
